# Executive Summary Dashboard

## Dashboard Purpose
Provide senior management with high-level overview of system performance and business health.

## Target Audience
- Senior Management
- Finance Directors
- Department Heads

## Refresh Frequency
- Real-time for KPIs
- Daily for trend data
- Weekly for summaries
